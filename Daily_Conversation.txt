*** Daily 10 English Conversation Sentences ****************************************
************************************************************************************

******* 1st Day ********************************************************************
******* 08-Sep-17 ******************************************************************
************************************************************************************

1. You scoundrel. - tumi bodmas.
2. What a big devil! - koto boro shoytan re baba.
3. I will slap off your teeth. - thappor diye dat fele debo.
4. I will make you flat by beating. - tomake pitiye soja kore debo.
5. Keep your voice down. - gola namiye kotha bolo.
6. Keep your eyes down. - cokh namiye kotha bolo.
7. Don't show your temper to me. - amar dike cokh pakiyo na.
8. I will dip you into dirty water. - tomake pocha panite cubiye debo. 
9. I will teach you a good lesson. - tomake ucit shikkha debo.
10. I will kick you out from here. - tomake lathi diye ber kore debo ekhan theke.

******* 2nd Day ********************************************************************
******* 09-Sep-17 ******************************************************************
************************************************************************************

1. You hippocrates. - tumi vondo.
2. I will see you my foot. - tomake dekhe nebo.
3. Keep your finger down. - angul namiye kotha bolo.
4. Mind your language. - vasa songjoto koro.
5. Hold your tongue. - mukh samle kotha bolo. 
6. Don't grow so far. - besi bar bero na.
7. Mind your own business. - nijer corkay tel dao.
8. What do you think of yourself? - tumi nijeke ki mone koro? 
9. Do you think that I am a fool? - tumi ki mone koro ami ekta boka?
10. Don't cross your limit. - sima cariye zeona. 

******* 3rd Day ********************************************************************
******* 13-Sep-17 ******************************************************************
************************************************************************************

1. You cheat! - tumi protarok!
2. Have you gone mad? - tumi ki pagol hoye geco?
3. You won't get away more. - tumi ar par pabena.
4. Who on earth did this? - kon gadha eta korecilo? 
5. What the hell are you doing? - ki ghorar dim korco?
6. O! I am undone. - mago! gelam.
7. Don't show your audacity. - spordha dekhio na.
8. For shame! You have done this! - chi! chi! tomar emon kaj!
9. You devil! Hold your tongue before speaking. - ore pasondo! mukh samle kotha bolis.
10. Pish! What insolence! - ishh! aspordha!
